A smarter way to manage your engagement workflow.

Secure, cloud-based client collaboration and project management designed for accounting firms.

Featured In: Accounting Today Quartz Money 20/20 Forrester

Auvenir is building a technology-driven cloud-based platform designed specifically for small to medium sized accounting firms. Through a more efficient engagement workflow, Auvenir helps firms save time and offer their clients a customized, branded and more collaborative experience from anywhere, on your computer, smartphone, or other device

What the platform offers

An easy-to-use, custom branded portal for your clients An all-in-one workflow solution for your firm A secure experience for everyone

An easy-to-use, custom branded portal for your clients

Clients can access information and tasks assigned to them so they can quickly understand what items to provide, securely upload documents, and add comments in one place.

An all-in-one workflow solution for your firm

Complete engagements faster and continually improve your workflow by combining planning, secure document management, communication, and reporting tools all in one solution.

A secure experience for everyone

We know data security is one of your top priorities. That’s why we built security into the platform, so you can confidently access your engagements anytime and anywhere.
